Esports Summer Camp

Are you interested in Esports?

Do you play games competitively?

Are you all about cracking the new META?

If so, we want you. This summer, June 7-14, 2020, Miami University’s Varsity Esports Team is proud to present our second annual Esports Summer Camp on the Miami University campus in Oxford, OH. Come learn about the world of Esports and compete in nightly camp tournaments to develop skills, meet new friends, and learn about potential careers in Esports, games and marketing.

For $1100, you’ll spend a week living at Miami, just a short walk from King Library, the home of the Varsity Esports Arena and Miami’s Games Lab. Camp fees include all meals, lodging and cover fees for all activities. Programming includes daily classes on the history, culture and business of Esports, a LAN event every night focusing on a specific genre of games complete with tournaments, daily physical training just like our varsity players, and guest appearances by team members and industry partners. Network, play, and learn in our state-of-the-art facilities from the nationally ranked faculty, staff and students that built the first Division 1 Varsity Esports team.

Everyone is welcome, age 12-18. No level of game knowledge or experience is necessary, but we will have opportunities for talented players to compete against some of our Varsity finest.

If you’d like to know more about the camp, contact Phill Alexander at phill.alexander@miamioh.edu
