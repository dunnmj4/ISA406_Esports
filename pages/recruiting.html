Why Miami?

Miami's Varsity Esports has stood out as one of the leading pioneers in the collegiate ESports community. Since it's creation, the program has stood as among one of the best, receiving national recognition. Our young program has already compiled many achievements, including winning the inaugural National Association of Collegiate Esports Overwatch Championship. Our program prides itself in helping players to develop and hone their skills as players to compete amongst the top national competition. In addition to our five varsity squads, our Hatchery features players training and fine-tuning skills to prepare for the varsity level.

In addition to competing with the top collegiate Esports players in the country, our academic programs in the Department of Emerging Technologies in Business and Design are nationally acclaimed, preparing students to enter this exploding industry. The department is committed to providing opportunities that interactive media present through hands-on, interactive and project-based courses and experiences, where students are encouraged to experiment, innovate and collaborate. We offer undergraduate, graduate, and graduate-certificate programs.

Excited to get involved? Miami University’s Varsity Esports Team proudly hosts our Esports Summer Camp on the Miami University Oxford Campus. From July 7-14th 2020, you can come learn about the world of Esports and compete in nightly camp tournaments to develop skills, meet new friends, and learn about potential careers in Esports, games and marketing. To learn more about our camp, visit our camp website.
